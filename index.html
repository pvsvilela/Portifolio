About

Grew at the intersection of technology and design

I grew professionally at the intersection of technology and design. I started in software development (React, .NET, Python, Azure), but quickly discovered that what truly drives me are the people behind the screens. That led me naturally to UX and UI, where logic, sensitivity, and creativity meet.

I translate complex problems into simple, intuitive, coherent experiences. I enjoy understanding how people think and behave when using digital products — and I’m passionate about the micro-interactions that silently improve an interface.

My daily work blends research, prototyping, testing, refinement, and strong collaboration with product and engineering teams. My technical background allows me to act as a bridge between designers and developers.

As a UX/UI Analyst Pleno, my goal is to create accessible, functional, meaningful digital experiences guided by empathy, purpose, and detail.
